Admissions

Start your journey to becoming an engineering leader

Application Process

Follow these simple steps to join our engineering community

1

Submit Application

Complete our online application form with your personal and academic information.

2

Submit Documents

Upload your transcripts, test scores, and any required supporting documents.

3

Interview

Participate in an admission interview (virtual or in-person) with our faculty.

4

Receive Decision

Get your admission decision within 4-6 weeks of completing your application.

Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 on 4.0 scale
  • SAT score of 1200+ or ACT score of 25+
  • Completion of prerequisite courses:
    • Mathematics (through Calculus)
    • Physics
    • Chemistry
  • Personal statement and letters of recommendation

  • Bachelor's degree in engineering or related field
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 on 4.0 scale
  • GRE scores (optional for 2024)
  • Statement of purpose
  • Three letters of recommendation
  • Resume/CV

  • All standard admission requirements
  • TOEFL score of 80+ or IELTS 6.5+
  • Transcript evaluation for non-US degrees
  • Financial documentation
  • Passport copy
